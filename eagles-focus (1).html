Why 60/10?

Classic pomodoro uses 25/5, but deep work — studying dense material, writing, coding — often needs longer runway to reach flow. The 60-minute focus / 10-minute break rhythm gives you time to sink in, while the break is long enough to genuinely reset your attention.

The long break

After every 4 pomodoros (editable in Settings) you earn a 40-minute long break. Use it away from the screen: eat, walk, stretch, nap. Recovery is part of training — ask any athlete.

Rules of the huddle

One task per pomodoro. Phone out of reach. If a distraction pops into your head, jot it down and return to the play. An interrupted pomodoro doesn't count — protect the hour like a lead in the fourth quarter.

Make it yours

All durations, auto-start behavior, and sound are editable in Settings. The game credits focus minutes, so custom lengths still count fairly toward your 50 hours.
